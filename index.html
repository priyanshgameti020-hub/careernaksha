About Us

CSERF is a non-political, non-profit, non-government foundation to promote career research & development activities for youth. Our social work addresses issues in the sustainable education & skill development sectors.

Our CSR vision is to build a foundation with rock-solid, sustainable goals to provide support, assistance and growth to students & youths of India. India is the next superpower and we as an organization want to work towards education, skill development, career counselling, research & development and be part of India's journey of becoming a developed nation by 2050.

Our Vision

We are striving to create a society where every socio-economically deprived youth will get an opportunity to access topmost career counselling, guidance & skill development support.

Our Mission

To empower socio-economically deprived, marginalized rural youth and students with low family income by guiding them to build their careers in the right direction and creating a conducive environment for their career development.

Our Outcomes

Since our founding, CSERF has conducted impactful career guidance interventions across Gujarat and other parts of India, directly reaching thousands of students in government schools, colleges, and community centres.

Our programs have delivered measurable improvements in career awareness, psychometric self-understanding, and higher-education enrollment rates — especially among students from rural and low-income backgrounds.

We measure our success by the positive change we bring to every student we serve — helping them set meaningful career goals and take concrete steps toward achieving them.
